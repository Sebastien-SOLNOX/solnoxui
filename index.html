What we do

Engineering services for the energy transition.

From government-mandated sustainability reporting to off-grid power design, Solnox Systems helps companies turn environmental goals, compliance requirements, and energy storage opportunities into practical projects.

01

Sustainability Reporting

Support for companies working toward government-mandated sustainability goals, including practical reporting structures and evidence-ready documentation.

02

Off-grid Power Systems

Design support for compliant energy systems that reduce reliance on the grid and suit the realities of remote, commercial, and community sites.

03

Lithium Battery Lifecycle

Safer planning for lithium battery use, transport, storage, and disposal, with a focus on risk, compliance, and lifecycle responsibility.

04

Automation and VPPs

Automated monitoring, control logic, and virtual power plant thinking for energy assets that need to work together intelligently.

05

Rebates and Grants

Help identifying and preparing for energy storage rebates, grants, and incentives so projects can move with stronger commercial footing.

06

Portable Off-grid Systems

Small, practical power systems for temporary, mobile, and remote use cases where clean independence matters.

How we work

Clear enough for compliance. Practical enough for the field.

Compliance aware. We design with reporting, standards, safety, and approval pathways in mind from the start.

Site realistic. We consider how systems will be installed, monitored, transported, maintained, and eventually retired.

Lifecycle focused. We account for batteries, infrastructure, data, and operational responsibility beyond the first day of use.
